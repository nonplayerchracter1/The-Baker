Our Story

Family tradition since 1975

Founded by a Cagayan De Oro City Family in 2003, The Baker began as a humble neighbourhood panaderia. For three generations, we've been dedicated to the craft of baking, blending cherished Filipino recipes with refined European techniques to create something uniquely delicious.

While we honour traditions like slow fermentation and baking in banana leaves, our philosophy is rooted in fusion. We believe the rich, earthy flavours of local ingredients like muscovado sugar, coconut, and purple yam (ube) pair beautifully with classic buttery pastry methods.

Our Signature Specialties

Unique creations that define The Baker

Ube Croissant

Our signature fusion: a classic, buttery French croissant swirled with sweet, vibrant purple yam (ube) filling. A perfect blend of European technique and iconic Filipino flavour.

Spanish Bread

A beloved Filipino bakery staple. These soft, fluffy rolls are filled with a sweet, milky filling, rolled in breadcrumbs, and baked to perfection. Enjoyed daily across the islands.

Puto Bumbong

A festive, traditional delicacy. Purple glutinous rice is steamed in bamboo tubes, then topped with butter, grated coconut, and muscovado sugar. A taste of a Filipino Christmas.
